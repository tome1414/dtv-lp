Why Golf Qualifies for a DTV Visa

DTV Visa

Thailand's long-stay visa
launched in July 2024

  • Valid for 5 years · Multiple entry
  • Up to 180 days per stay (+180-day extension available)
  • Application fee approx. USD 350
  • Required bank balance 500,000 THB (approx. USD 14,000+)
  • Sports, culture & wellness are accepted application categories

Thailand's Soft Power Policy
officially recognises golf

The DTV Visa's "Sports category" requires participation in a sport activity recognised by Thailand's Ministry of Sport. Our partner golf school has been inspected and confirmed by the Thai Ministry of Sport as a fully certified facility.

We partner exclusively with Thailand PGA-certified teaching pros. The official acceptance letters they issue fully satisfy Thai government requirements, and our agents handle everything from document preparation to submission.
